Understanding the Profiles

The Witness & Whistleblower Databasedocuments the individuals who have significantly contributed to the public understanding of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP).

Profiles include firsthand witnesses, whistleblowers, scientists, investigators, journalists, attorneys, military personnel, government officials, and other contributors whose documented work has influenced investigations, congressional oversight, scientific research, or public disclosure.

Each profile explains why the individual is included and documents their connections to related hearings, organizations, cases, documents, interviews, legislation, publications, timeline events, and other entities throughout the GreyAlien knowledge base.
