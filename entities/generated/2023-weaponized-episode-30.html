WEAPONIZED Episode #30 — David Grusch, Whistleblower Retaliation, and the Attempt to Discredit His Testimony

Jeremy Corbell and George Knapp examine efforts to discredit David Grusch after his public testimony, including reporting about his prior PTSD treatment, and discuss retaliation, whistleblower protections, and the potential chilling effect on other witnesses.
