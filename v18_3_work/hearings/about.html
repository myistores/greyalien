About GreyAlien

Connecting the People, Events, Documents, and Discoveries Shaping Our Understanding of UAPs.

GreyAlien explores science, space, technology and unexplained phenomena through a structured, source-conscious knowledge base.

More than a news site

News is important, but context is more valuable. GreyAlien is designed to connect current developments with historical cases, testimony, official records, researchers, interviews, podcasts, films and scientific discoveries.

A knowledge graph

Each page is treated as part of a larger network. Hearings connect to witnesses. Witnesses connect to interviews. Interviews connect to podcasts. Historical cases connect to government documents and research. These relationships help visitors move through the subject naturally while preserving context.

Editorial approach

The long-term goal is to clearly distinguish primary sources, verified facts, reported claims, interpretation and open questions. AI may assist with organization, cross-referencing and research, but published material remains subject to human review.
